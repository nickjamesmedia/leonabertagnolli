About Leona

Leona Bertagnolli has lived, worked, farmed, and raised her family in Clearwater County for over three decades. She believes in practical, commonsense decisions that support agriculture and small business while protecting what makes our communities special.

Her priorities are straightforward: listen to residents, keep council accountable, and focus on responsible growth so Clearwater County stays a place we’re proud to call home.
