Thank You, Clearwater County Ward 3

Thank you to everyone in Ward 3 who took the time to talk with me, share concerns, and cast their vote in the 2025 municipal election.

While I was not successful in this election, I'm grateful for the support, conversations, and engagement from residents across the county. It was an honour to put my name forward and advocate for responsible growth, transparency, and a strong future for Clearwater County.

At this time, I'll be stepping back from public campaigning to focus on family and farm life. I encourage residents to stay informed, involved, and engaged with their local government moving forward.

Thank you again for your support.

- Leona Bertagnolli
